Have you...

ever wanted to learn to program but thought that the barrier to entry was just too high?
Has a lack of money and/or resources stopped you from trying to level up your life with coding?
Don't Need Money To Code is a free resource housing lectures, in-person workshops and free resources for people from all walks of life and all backgrounds.
DNMTC is designed to meet you where you're at and get you to where you want to be.
